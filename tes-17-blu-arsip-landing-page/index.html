BlueArchive

Blue Archive is a school monogatari RPG developed by NAT Games, subsidiary of Korean company Nexon and released by Yostar, developer of Arknights and Azur Lane. The game is available on Android and iOS in Japanese. ‎The story begins in a school city named “Teyvat”. Players will act as the consultant teacher and lead the students to solve the crises in the city. The game provides an auto battle feature with 3D scenes and models. During battle, players can bring along 4 main characters and 2 supporting characters. It is the players’ job to unleash their skill and lead them to victory. Aside from normal missions, there are also different contents such as arrest warrant missions, special commissions, PVP and strategic battle. When players develop relationships with the characters, they can unlock character stories with CG and 2D animation too.
